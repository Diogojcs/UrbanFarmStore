Help & FAQs

We’re here to help! Below you will find answers to some frequently asked questions about our services, shipping, and more. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

General Questions

Urban Farm Store is your trusted provider of locally grown, sustainable urban farm products. We offer fresh, pesticide-free produce sourced from urban farms, promoting healthy eating and a greener planet.

You can easily place an order through our online store. Simply browse our selection, add items to your cart, and complete the checkout process. We offer various payment and delivery options to meet your needs.

While our produce is not certified organic, all our products are grown without the use of synthetic pesticides or harmful chemicals, ensuring fresh and eco-friendly food.

Shipping & Delivery

We offer local delivery within the city and surrounding areas. You can choose from standard delivery or express delivery during checkout. Same-day delivery is available for certain locations.

Most orders are delivered within 1-3 business days, depending on your location. Express delivery orders are typically delivered within 24 hours. Delivery times may vary during peak seasons or due to unforeseen circumstances.

Yes, we offer free shipping on orders over a certain amount, typically $50 or more. You will see if you qualify for free shipping during the checkout process.
